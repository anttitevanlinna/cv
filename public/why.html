Why

It's hip to do an app as a CV. While LinkedIn can tell a lot, it doesn't quite convey what can one do. The limitations of traditional CV format are quite huge. Making an app that does cool stuff can much better showcase your talents. This site is an app that will handle the journey of getting to know Antti's history, personality, and achievements. The BIO section gives a few links to the stuff that I've been working with over the years.
